Gifts

If you would like to give us a gift, we would be grateful if you followed the Chinese tradition of money in a red envelope (Hongbao). These red packets are given as a gesture of good fortune and prosperity for the bride and groom.

Questions and Answers

Can I invite extra people?

Only people that received an invite can attend. The venue has a strict limit, and anyone not on the invite list will not be allowed in.

What is the dress code?

The dress code for our wedding is formal. Additionally, please don't wear red.

How many people will be attending the wedding?

60 guests are expected to attend the wedding. This is a small and intimate wedding with close family and friends.

Can I book accommodation at the venue?

We have a number of rooms reserved for our guests. Please contact us if you're interested.

Can I stay at the venue the night before?

There's a wedding at the venue the night before, so unlikely for there to be spare rooms. This will be confirmed closer to the date.

What are the suggestions for accommodations and transportation in the area?

Click on the icons in the Google Map above, each icon has a description with recommendation information. Click on this link for direct access to the map.

Will there be transport or parking?

Parking is available at the venue for all guests. There is no organised transport, but it is possible to get close to the venue via public buses. Taxis are recommended when going home.

What’s on the menu?

Food will be served buffet style, multiple times throughout the day. Exact menu details will be sent closer to the time. Please get in contact if you have dietary requirements.

What’s the situation with the bar?

A welcome drink, drink with the meal, and a toast drink will be provided for all guests. After that, there will be a bar available for the guests to purchase drinks.

How and when to RSVP?

RSVP by replying to the message sent with your invitation or contacting us directly. Please RVSP ASAP and no later than the 8th Feb 2026.

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

The wedding will be mainly indoors, with lots of outdoor green space for guests to enjoy and take pictures.

After the wedding, how can I share pictures I took?

There will be a photographer and videographer at the wedding. Please don't feel forced to take too many pictures. However, we will soon have a QR code available for you to upload and share any pictures you take.

Who should I contact with additional questions?

Additional questions and answers will be added over time. But please contact us directly if you have any other questions.
